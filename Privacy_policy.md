Privacy Policy for Flourish
Flourish ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Flourish mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App and the choices you have associated with that data.
1. Information We Collect
We collect several types of information to provide and improve our service:
Personal Information:
Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we collect your email address, display name, and profile photo URL.
Habit Data: Information about the habits you are tracking, including habit name, cost per use, and related savings calculations.
Progress Data: Your savings progress, streaks, achievements, and tree growth data.
Social Content: Posts, comments, and interactions you create within the App's social features.
Usage Data:
We may collect information about how you access and use the App. This may include device information, IP address, app version, and usage patterns. This data is collected through Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics, but only with your explicit consent.
2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information for various purposes:
To provide and maintain our service
To authenticate your account and manage your profile
To store and synchronize your data across devices using Firebase
To calculate and display your savings progress and achievements
To enable social features, including leaderboards and social feed
To improve and optimize the App's performance (with your consent)
To detect, prevent, and address technical issues and crashes (with your consent)
To comply with legal obligations
3. Data Storage and Third-Party Services
We use Google Firebase services to store and process your data:
Firebase Authentication: Handles user authentication via Google Sign-In
Cloud Firestore: Stores your account data, habits, trees, achievements, and social content
Firebase Analytics: Collects usage analytics (only with your explicit consent)
Firebase Crashlytics: Collects crash reports and error logs (only with your explicit consent)
Firebase App Check: Protects your data from unauthorized access
Your data is stored securely on Google's servers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, but no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.
4. User Consent and Control
You have control over certain data collection:
Analytics Consent: You can opt-in or opt-out of Firebase Analytics data collection through the App's settings. By default, analytics are disabled until you provide consent.
Crashlytics Consent: You can opt-in or opt-out of crash reporting through the App's settings. By default, crash reporting is disabled until you provide consent.
Account Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the App's profile settings. This will permanently delete your account, habits, progress, and social content.
5. Data Sharing and Disclosure
We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:
Public Leaderboards: Your display name, profile photo, savings milestones, and achievements may be visible to other users on public leaderboards if you choose to participate.
Social Features: Posts and comments you create in the App's social feed are visible to other users.
Service Providers: We share data with Google Firebase to provide our services. Firebase is bound by Google's privacy policies.
Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.
6. Data Retention
We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information, except where we are required to retain it by law or for legitimate business purposes.
7. Your Rights
Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:
Right to Access: You can access your personal data through the App's profile and settings screens.
Right to Rectification: You can update your display name and profile information at any time.
Right to Erasure: You can delete your account and all associated data through the App's settings.
Right to Data Portability: You can export your data through the App's profile settings.
Right to Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw consent for analytics and crash reporting at any time through the App's settings.
8. Children's Privacy
Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.
9. International Data Transfers
Your information may be transferred to and maintained on computers located outside of your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where data protection laws may differ. By using our App, you consent to the transfer of your information to Google's servers, which may be located in various countries.
10. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
11. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App's feedback and support features or via the contact information provided in the App.
